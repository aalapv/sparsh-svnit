Lights, Camera, Action

A stock market game where you have set up of a virtual stock market.

    • Number of team members: 3

    • Third Round: Voice the Video
    • Number of team members: 3

4. Tribal Hunt

Find your kin to make you win!

Participants are divided into groups of ten and sent to different locations. Each person is given a family name and they have to find their whole family after which clues will be given to them to reach other locations, perform tasks and the first group to reach the final location wins.

  • Rules:
    • 2 -3 slots
    • 70 - 80 people per slot

5. Catalyst

A mock CAT consisting of high-standard questions.

  • Rules:
    • Individual participation

6. Treasure Hunt

Reach the treasure, sweet the pleasure!

  • Rules:
    • First round: Solve the puzzle
    • Second round: Find the things
    • Third round: Find the treasure
    • Number of team members: 4

7. 50 Ka Funda

The team gets an initial investment of Rs 50. The team is expected to start a virtual company and come up with a product or a service with the initial investment of Rs. 50 that they can sell and make a profit on.

  • Rules:
    • Number of team members: 4

8. JAM- Just A Minute

It's all about speaking. But, in a minute.

  • Rules:
    • Round 1: Talk anything about the picked topic for 1 minute.
    • Round 2: A funny topic is given, talk about it in a serious way.
    • Round 3: A serious topic is given, talk about it humorously.
    • Individual participation

9. Managerial Roadies

A corporate crisis will be stormed upon the participants and they will have to provide apt solutions for the same. The level of difficulty increases with each round.

  • Rules:
    • Number of team members: 3
