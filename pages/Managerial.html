4. Tribal Hunt

  • Statement-Find your kin to make you win.
  • Individual participation
  • 2-3 slots, 70-80 people per slot
  • People divided into groups of 10 and sent to different locations. Each person is given a family name and they have to find their whole family after which clues will be given to them to reach other locations perform tasks and the first group to reach the final location wins.
