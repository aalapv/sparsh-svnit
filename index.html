About Sparsh

Sparsh is the annual techno-cultural festival of Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology. It usually takes place in the second or third week of February, and is one of the largest of its kind in the western Region. This five-day festival consists of mega-attractions as well as informal, technical & managerial events among numerous other competitions.
